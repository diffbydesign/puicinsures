Our Story

Eight decades of
standing by you.

Pacific Union Insurance Company was founded on June 5, 1945 by Don Aurelio Periquet Sr. — a visionary pioneer of the Philippine insurance industry.

We received Certificate of Authority No. 1 from the President of the Philippines, making us the very first non-life insurer authorized to operate in the country.

Our inaugural bond was issued on behalf of former President Jose P. Laurel Sr. in favor of the Commonwealth of the Philippines — a distinction that speaks to the trust placed in us from day one.

That same warmth and integrity guides every policy we write today.
