Our Story

Eight decades of
standing by you.

Pacific Union Insurance Company was founded on June 5, 1945 by Don Aurelio Periquet Sr. — a visionary pioneer of the Philippine insurance industry.

We received Certificate of Authority No. 1 from the President of the Philippines — making us the very first non-life insurer authorized to operate in the nation.

Our inaugural bond was issued on behalf of former President Jose P. Laurel Sr. in favor of the Commonwealth of the Philippines. That level of trust has defined us for 80 years — and still does today.
